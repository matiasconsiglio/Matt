MaTT

Matias Consiglio

Chilean/Italian Dj

Malmö - Sweden


4 years since I got my first controller, a Traktor Kontrol S4. I Started the journey of Djing playing Tech House. As the years passed by I've been able to travel the decks by playing House, Progressive House and now I'm getting curious about Minimal Techno. Took me time to learn and I still have a long journey of learning to go, but happy to do so.


I could call myself a dj from the new times since technology offers a lot of interesting possibilities, like preparing the sets and also while playing them. I'm really energetic and every time I have the chance to play I try to do my best while always having a great time. I fell in love with looping and experimenting with the different options of the mixer. I try at most times to have both tracks playing and make sets connect with different but similar genres like storytelling.


In my time in Chile I managed to play in some clubs in the capital. Here in Sweden I've managed to play in some private parties and also raves. Recently I was invited to be part of a small collective where we share a studio. It has helped me to start making my own portfolio and share what I love to do. I look forward to play, share and connect with more people around Malmö and the world.
